Contact and Location

Driving directions

  • From Hwy 113 proceed west on Hutchison Drive
  • Pass through the traffic circle and turn left on Campbell Rd.
  • On Campbell Rd drive 0.3 miles to Garrod Dr. Turn right on Garrod Dr.
  • On Garrod Dr. drive 0.3 miles to where the road forks. Keep to the right on Garrod Dr. Ignore fork at Environmental Services Lane.
  • After the fork drive 0.3 miles on Garrod Dr. to the parking lot of the Aquatic Biology and Environmental Sciences building.
  • CABA facility offices are just inside the door in Rm. 103 and 105
